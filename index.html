Trust and protection

Professional service.
Controlled disclosure.

Good Remedy starts with a plain-language work story, not a demand for sensitive files or a method worksheet. We help shape one bounded first review while protecting your information and our behind-the-scenes review system.

Start without oversharing.
For the first conversation, describe the workflow in general terms. A redacted, synthetic, public-source, or representative example is enough to start.
We do not need the whole operation.
Good Remedy looks for one bounded place to begin: a draft, message, campaign, handoff, decision point, or workflow sample that represents the issue.
The packet is a review aid.
A Good Remedy packet does not approve, certify, authorize sending, replace required professional review, or replace the person responsible for the decision.

First-review boundary: Please do not submit confidential client records, private personal information, legal, HR, clinical, financial, regulated, privileged, customer-record, identifying, credential, or other sensitive material through the public form. If a workflow involves sensitive material, start with a non-sensitive description and we can discuss a safer scoping path.
